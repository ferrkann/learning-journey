# Assets

This folder stores supporting files used across the repository: images, diagrams, PDFs, and other attachments.

**TL;DR (ID):** `/assets` berisi file pendukung (gambar, diagram, PDF, dll.) yang dipakai oleh catatan di `/notes`, ringkasan di `/topics`, atau mini output di `/projects`.

---

## What goes here

- Images: screenshots, photos, figures, diagrams (`.png`, `.jpg`, `.svg`)
- PDFs: exported summaries or compiled documents (`.pdf`)
- Attachments: small supporting files that help explain notes/projects

---

## Suggested organization

Use subfolders to keep it clean:

- `assets/images/` — figures, diagrams, screenshots
- `assets/pdfs/` — exported PDFs (monthly wrap-ups, compiled notes)
- `assets/diagrams/` — source diagrams (if separate from images)
- `assets/misc/` — other small attachments

Example:
- `assets/images/2025-12/`
- `assets/pdfs/2025-12-learning-wrapup.pdf`

---

## Naming convention

Keep filenames descriptive and consistent:

- `YYYY-MM-DD_topic_short-desc.png`
- `YYYY-MM_topic_wrapup.pdf`

Examples:
- `2025-12-28_structural-geology_intro-diagram.png`
- `2025-12_learning-wrapup.pdf`

---

## Notes

- Avoid uploading copyrighted raw materials (e.g., full book scans or paid course slides).
- Large files should be kept minimal to avoid bloating the repo.
